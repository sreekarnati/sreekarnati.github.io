Featured Projects & Impact

Zomato Restaurant Market Strategy

Python • Pandas • Seaborn • Market Analysis

The Problem: Identifying the key drivers of restaurant success (high ratings/volume) in a crowded market to inform new investment strategies.

The Challenge: The raw dataset was inconsistent, with messy text in 'cuisine' and 'cost' fields. This required advanced regex and data transformation to make it usable for analysis.

The Impact: Discovered that specialized restaurants (1-2 cuisines) rate 15% higher than generalists. Identified "Online Ordering" as a critical success factor, recommending it as a mandatory feature for new entrants.

Superstore Profitability Dashboard

Tableau • Business Intelligence • Profit Audit

The Problem: Sales were growing, but overall profit was stagnant. Management needed immediate insight into the profit leak without digging through spreadsheets.

My Approach: Built an interactive, drill-down Tableau dashboard to audit profit margins by region and product. Created calculated fields to measure discount effectiveness and a parameter to toggle views.

The Impact: Pinpointed Texas and Pennsylvania as major loss centers due to high discounts on 'Tables'. Recommended a localized pricing strategy estimated to recover 10% of lost profits annually.

Walmart Weekly Sales Forecasting

Python • Prophet • Time-Series • Inventory Planning

The Problem: Inaccurate inventory planning due to complex seasonal sales spikes (especially around holidays) leading to either stockouts or high holding costs.

The Challenge: Traditional models struggled to parse multiple seasonalities (weekly, yearly, holiday). External factors like CPI and unemployment also impacted sales, requiring feature engineering.

The Impact: Developed a model using Facebook Prophet that accurately predicts sales peaks, enabling store managers to optimize stock levels and potentially reducing holding costs by improving order lead times.

Titanic Survival Analysis (EDA)

Python • Pandas • Seaborn • Statistical Inference

The Problem: Quantify the socio-economic factors that determined survival rates on the Titanic, identifying if specific demographics were systematically favored.

The Challenge: A significant portion of 'Age' data was missing, which could skew the analysis. I had to develop a logical imputation strategy based on passenger class and title (e.g., 'Master').

The Impact: Confirmed statistically significant correlations: Females had a 74% survival rate vs. 19% for males. This project demonstrated foundational skills in data cleaning, imputation, and using visualization for evidence-based conclusions.

Netflix Content Trends Analysis (EDA)

Python • Pandas • Categorical Data Analysis

The Problem: Analyze Netflix's content investment strategy by tracking library composition, genre popularity, and regional production over time.
