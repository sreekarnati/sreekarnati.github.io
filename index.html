Featured Projects & Impact

Zomato Restaurant Market Strategy

Python • Pandas • Seaborn • Market Analysis

The Problem: Identifying the key drivers of restaurant success (high ratings/volume) in a crowded market to inform new investment strategies.

My Approach: Cleaned and analyzed a dataset of 7,000+ restaurants. Engineered a 'Cuisine Count' feature to test if specialization correlates with ratings. Used regex for advanced text cleaning.

The Impact: Discovered that specialized restaurants (1-2 cuisines) rate 15% higher than generalists. Identified "Online Ordering" as a critical success factor, recommending it as a mandatory feature for new entrants.

Superstore Profitability Dashboard

Tableau • Business Intelligence • Profit Audit

The Problem: Sales were growing, but overall profit was stagnant. Management needed immediate insight into the profit leak.

My Approach: Built an interactive, drill-down Tableau dashboard to audit profit margins by region and product. Created calculated fields to measure discount effectiveness.

The Impact: Pinpointed Texas and Pennsylvania as major loss centers due to high discounts on 'Tables'. Recommended a localized pricing strategy estimated to recover 10% of lost profits annually.

Walmart Weekly Sales Forecasting

Python • Prophet • Time-Series • Inventory Planning

The Problem: Inaccurate inventory planning due to complex seasonal sales spikes (especially around holidays) leading to either stockouts or high holding costs.

My Approach: Applied the Facebook Prophet model to automatically capture weekly, yearly, and holiday seasonality. Engineered features for external economic factors like CPI.

The Impact: Developed a model that accurately predicts sales peaks, enabling store managers to optimize stock levels and potentially reducing holding costs by improving order lead times.

Titanic Survival Analysis

Python • Pandas • Statistical Inference

The Problem: Quantify the socio-economic factors that determined survival rates on the Titanic, identifying if specific demographics were systematically favored.

My Approach: Performed extensive data imputation for missing age values based on passenger class. Used Seaborn to visualize survival rates by gender, class, and fare.

The Impact: Confirmed statistically significant correlations: Females had a 74% survival rate vs. 19% for males. Demonstrated foundational skills in data cleaning, imputation, and using visualization for evidence-based conclusions.
